Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need my own API keys?
Yes, you'll need to connect your own API keys from providers like OpenAI, Anthropic, or Google. This means you only pay for what you use, and your keys stay private and secure.
What counts as one session?
One session is a complete conversation with your chosen topic and AI models, regardless of how many rounds or models you use.
Can I cancel anytime?
Yes, you can cancel your Pro subscription anytime. You'll keep Pro access until the end of your billing period.
Do unused free sessions roll over?
No, free sessions reset at the beginning of each month. Pro users have unlimited sessions so this doesn't apply.
Is my data secure?
Yes! All API keys are encrypted using industry-standard AES-256 encryption. They're only used server-side to make API calls and are never exposed to your browser. Read our Privacy Policy for full details.
